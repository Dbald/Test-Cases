NEXT GEN INTERACTIVE SPATIAL PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Projects are complex and fast-moving. Let Mixx Reality help your projects come alive - so collaborators can quickly find what they need, easily connect the dots & visually highlight what’s important, and communicate & action faster.

MISSION

Mixx Reality is the smartest way to spatially manage your complex projects. Our AI-powered platform help you connect data and collaborators in interactive, visual, intelligent spaces that highlight the right information, at the right time, to the right stakeholders - across your entire project.

PROBLEM

Modern enterprise projects are complex, dynamic, and highly collaborative. Existing project management processes (designed to be executed by people) are struggling to keep up with the growing amount of data and platforms, ad-hoc communications, and in-the-moment decisions - often with significant downstream impact.

Companies are looking for better ways to keep up, and they are increasingly reaching for more visual and immersive ways to comprehend and to experience their projects, as they are happening.

SOLUTION

Mixx Reality offers a unique, all-in-one spatial, interactive, and intelligent project management platform specifically designed to tackle rapidly changing demands facing today’s projects.

What does it mean for our customer:

• Faster - a typical interactive use case can be delivered in hours or days, instead of months or years.

• Easier - By radically lowering the expertise required to “go visual”, non-technical, in-house resources can skip writing requirements, pulling data, and attending sign-off meetings; they simply build what they need.

• Cheaper - A typical customer can expect a price reduction from tens of thousands of dollars per use case, to tens of dollars per month.
